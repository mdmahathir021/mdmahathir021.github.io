AIAA Design/Build/Fly 2025–26
Design Subteam Lead · TEAM MIST · Wichita, Kansas, USA

DBF is the world's largest collegiate RC aircraft competition. I led the design subteam — aerodynamic analysis in XFLR5, wing and tail sizing, aerostructural trade studies balancing performance against structural weight and stiffness constraints.

The thing I'm most proud of is a parametric sizing script I built early in the process. Every design parameter — span, aspect ratio, tail volume coefficients, weight budgets — was wired together so that changing one propagated through everything automatically. The team stopped redoing calculations by hand. It just worked, and people relied on it.

The 17th and 18th global ranks in the technical report and proposal came from writing I led as primary author. For a team from Bangladesh competing internationally against 175 teams, those ranks mean something.
